Sora

About Sora

Sora is the main character of the video game Kingdom Hearts: a game made by Square Enix in collaboration with Disney. Sora, along with donald and goofy travels across worlds to save them from the ever approaching darkness

Sora's Characteristics

  1. Sora is often seen smiling and cheerful even as he explores unfamiliar worlds with Donald and Goofy
  2. Despite Sora's cheerful demeanor he knows when to get serious in situations where he must defend his friends and himself from the dangers of the darkness
  3. Despite Sora's small frame, he is an extremely skilled and agile fighter who wields the keyblade: a weapon with the ability to free the hearts of those taken by darkness
  4. Sora believes that his friends are his source of power and if he were to lose them, Sora wouldn't know what to do with himself so he stops at nothing to make sure his friends are kept safe
  1. Sora grew up on the Destiny Islands with his friends Kairi and Riku. However, a disaster caused their world to be taken by darkness temporarily seperating the trio
  2. Sora would wake up alone where he make many new friends such as Donald Duck, Goofy, and more!
  3. Thanks to the friends Sora made along the way Sora managed to find his childhood friends again and although they were seperated once again due to unforseen circumstances they promised to meet each other again
